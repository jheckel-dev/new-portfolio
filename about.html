About Me

I am a customer service oriented professional working towards starting their career in programming.

Currently I am employed by Apple in the Bellevue Square store. I am a specialist assisting customers in troubleshooting some fo their minor problems with their iPhones, iPads, and MacBooks. I also assist customers with purchasing new devices and ensure that they have the confidence to use their devices when they leave the store.

Prior to this I was employed by a small beverage distributor in Maine where I had the pleasure to work with some of the most interesting businesses and restaurants in a seasonal area that taught me countless things about the field of sales, problem solving, and what it really means to be a flexible customer representative.

Before this I was in college where I worked as a server and bartender at a fun sports bar in Pullman Washington and I also worked as a probation officer assistant for the Latah County Courthouse in Moscow Idaho. Working as a server in a college town really help me hone in my customer service skills by balancing multiple tasks at once while also providing customers with a very positive experience. As a probation officer assistant I had the pleasure of worknig with some amazing people and had a number of very rewarding experiences being a positive role model for kids who really needed it.

I am always interested in learning about new opportunities and can be reached through this portfolio, by email: jalen.heckel@icloud.com, through GitHub, or through LinkedIn.
